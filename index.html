ABOUT US

Welcome to the website of Aid Colombian Children, a non-profit organisation in Colombia dedicated exclusively to the benefit of children who suffer from malaria and who, as a result of drug wars,have been forced to leave their homes. Founded in 2022, Its objective is to provide assistance to these children through the provision of assistance related to their health and accomodation(housing) needs. Our mission is to help children recover from malaria by providing them with free medical care, free mosquito nets for school students and anti-malaria medication for small children as well as other information about how children can protect themselves from mosquitoes. The children we are helping are from the poorest villages of Colombia. Many of them are living with malaria and other diseases because there is no medicine for them in the village. We recognize their pain and help our children to find a safe home and an opportunity to heal, both physically and emotionally
